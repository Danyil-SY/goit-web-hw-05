Chat Commands

  • exchange [days] [currencies] - Get the exchange rates for the specified number of days and currencies.
  • Example: exchange 2 EUR USD - Get the exchange rates for EUR and USD for the last 2 days.

Available Currencies

  • USD - US Dollar
  • EUR - Euro
  • GBP - British Pound
  • CHF - Swiss Franc
  • JPY - Japanese Yen
  • CAD - Canadian Dollar
  • AUD - Australian Dollar
  • CNY - Chinese Yuan
  • PLN - Polish Zloty
  • RUB - Russian Ruble
